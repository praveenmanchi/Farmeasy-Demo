Our integrated weather monitoring stations provide hyperlocal atmospheric data, enabling accurate forecasts and automated responses to changing conditions.

## Monitoring Parameters

- Temperature and humidity
- Precipitation tracking
- Wind speed and direction
- Solar radiation
- Atmospheric pressure
- Frost risk detection

## Forecasting Tools

- 7-day hyperlocal forecasts
- Severe weather alerts
- Growing degree day calculations
- Frost warnings
- Spray window identification
- Disease pressure models

## Automated Responses

- Irrigation adjustments
- Frost protection activation
- Harvest timing alerts
- Equipment scheduling
- Chemical application windows

## Data Integration

- Historical weather patterns
- Climate trend analysis
- Crop-specific recommendations
- Mobile notifications
- API access for custom integrations

Our weather stations provide farm-specific data that's significantly more accurate than regional forecasts, helping farmers make better operational decisions.
