**Sacramento, CA – November 28, 2024** – FarmEasy has been named to Fast Company's annual list of Most Innovative Companies in the Agriculture sector, highlighting the company's groundbreaking work in precision farming technology and commitment to environmental sustainability.

## Recognition Details

- **Innovation Excellence**: Ranked among the top 10 most innovative agricultural technology companies globally
- **Customer Impact**: Praised for measurable results in water conservation and yield improvement
- **Technology Leadership**: Recognized for AI-powered crop health prediction and off-grid solar sensor networks
- **Sustainability Focus**: Highlighted for helping farms reduce environmental impact while increasing profitability

"This recognition reflects our team's dedication to solving real farming challenges with practical, profitable technology," stated CTO Dr. Sarah Mitchell. "We are honored to be recognized by Fast Company for our innovation in sustainable agriculture."

The company's precision agriculture platform serves growers across diverse crop systems, providing data-driven insights that optimize resource use and increase yields while protecting the environment.

For more information about FarmEasy's technology and innovation, visit [farmeasy.com/innovation](https://farmeasy.com/innovation).

**About FarmEasy**  
FarmEasy is a leading agricultural IoT company specializing in precision farming, soil monitoring, and irrigation automation. With deployments across thousands of farms worldwide, the company helps growers increase profitability through sustainable, data-driven farming practices.
