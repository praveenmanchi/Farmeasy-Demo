## Project Overview

In a significant development for sustainable viticulture, **FarmEasy** has successfully deployed an advanced IoT sensor network at a 200-acre premium vineyard in Napa Valley, delivering a 40% reduction in water usage while simultaneously improving grape quality.

### Key Results

- 40% reduction in irrigation water consumption
- 15% improvement in grape sugar content consistency
- Real-time soil moisture monitoring across 50+ zones
- $85,000 annual savings in water and energy costs

## Impact and Technology

The installation includes 60 solar-powered sensor nodes measuring:

- Soil moisture at multiple depths
- Temperature profiles
- Electrical conductivity
- Real-time weather data

The system's **predictive irrigation algorithms** analyze historical trends, weather forecasts, and crop growth stages to optimize watering schedules automatically.
