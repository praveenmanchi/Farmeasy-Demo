## Program Overview

FarmEasy has launched a comprehensive internship program designed to train the next generation of agricultural technology professionals. The program provides hands-on experience in IoT engineering, data science, and precision agronomy. The inaugural class includes 18 interns from agricultural colleges and engineering schools across the country.

### Program Features

- Hands-on training in sensor deployment and field data collection
- Data science projects analyzing real farm datasets
- Mentorship from experienced agronomists and engineers
- For agricultural, engineering, and computer science students
- Field visits to customer farms across multiple growing regions
- Capstone project: Design and deploy a complete monitoring solution

### Intern Experiences

- Interns participated in sensor installation at a 500-acre vineyard
- Group project: Developed a machine learning model to predict irrigation needs
- Attended weekly seminars on sustainable agriculture and climate-smart farming
- Shadowed customer success team during farmer consultations

### How to Apply

Applications for the next internship cohort open in January. The program runs for 12 weeks during summer (June-August) with opportunities for extension. Interested students should submit a resume, cover letter, and academic transcript via the FarmEasy careers website.

### Program Benefits

- Competitive pay ($22-28/hour based on experience)
- Housing assistance for out-of-area interns
- Professional development workshops
- Networking with agricultural industry leaders
- Potential for full-time employment upon graduation

Over 75% of past interns have received full-time job offers from FarmEasy or partner organizations in the agtech sector.
